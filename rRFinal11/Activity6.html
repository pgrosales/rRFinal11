Activity 6: Persona Creation

Posted on: July 26,2019

This was our sixth activity in our User Design Class. This activity is connected to Activity 4: Need Finding, and Activity 5: Initial Prototype Login Forms. This activity is for us to create a imaginary User with specific narrative or situation, and how will the app will help their situation. This activity is good for us because we have a user point of view about the application.

Rose Lily is a student at a private school in Makati, She thinks that everyone's inclusion in reporting, can make the school better. This app can help here pursue her ideas about inclusion in reporting.
Her situation was she encountered a broken toilet flush, and she thinks that if she doesn't report it, the problem will just be harder to fix. With the use of CCTV she can take a photo of the broken flush, then fill in the necessary details about it, attach the picture, and choose the appropriate Office for the specific problem, then send.

Rock Jackson is raised with values and morals, he wants to spread his values to other, Integrity being one of his values, he wants to share his values with other people. His situation was when he was going back to the classroom, he spotted his classmates exchanging answer sheets which is a major offense, due to his belief in his values, he secretly took a photo. Later that day, he chose to do the right thing. With the use of CCTV he's going to report his classmates anonymously. First he filled in the necessary details, wrote his complaint, attached the photo he took, then he chose the Discipline office, then send.
