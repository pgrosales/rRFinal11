Activity 4: Need Finding

Posted on: July 12, 2019

This was our fourth activity, this activity is for us to find a scenario or a problem that we think can fix by creating an application considering all the aspects such as: Why does it need to be solved, How did we notice this? and What are the other solution we considered before choosing this solution.

Problem:

Students doesn’t keep track on their absences and tardiness. Inaccuracy in recording the attendance as per the student’s point of view. Some students tend to record/sign other students’ attendance even though they are not physically present.


Needs: Accurate recording of attendance (absences & tardiness) Attendance has a huge effect in student’s grade. Some subjects require attendance as 50% of student’s grade. Fair attendance records for those students who came in class on time.

Suggested Solution : CCTV- Catch, Catch, the Violator.
It is an application to help students/faculty members to report problems or issues faster than you ordinary face to face and email report.

General Objective To create a mobile and web application for the users to capture, compile, and send evidences about a problem.

Specific Objective • To maintain the quality and the effectiveness of the education/facilities. • To reduce the problems/issues that may have a long term effect of each individual. • To encourage students to help one another in reporting/solving problem.
